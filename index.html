About Conference

STRIDE Ethiopia is a pioneering annual event aimed at fostering collaboration and innovation in Science, Technology, Innovation (STI), and Digitalization. With a focus on national and international policy discussions, the event serves as a platform for government, private sector, academia, and development partners to converge and shape the digital economy landscape. Through exhibitions, forums, and networking opportunities, STRIDE Ethiopia facilitates engagement between stakeholders, promotes digital government services, supports startups, and showcases disruptive technologies. Join us in advancing Ethiopia's journey towards a vibrant and sustainable digital future
