About Conference

STRIDE Ethiopia, initiated by the Ministry of Innovation and Technology of Ethiopia, is a pioneering annual event aimed at fostering collaboration and innovation in Science, Technology, Research, Innovation, Digitalization, and Entrepreneurship (STRIDE). It serves as both a conference and an expo, bringing together stakeholders from startups, ecosystem builders, corporations, government offices, academia, and development partners. With a focus on national and international policy discussions, the event provides a platform to shape Ethiopia's digital economy landscape. Through exhibitions, forums, and networking opportunities, STRIDE Ethiopia facilitates engagement, promotes digital government services, supports startups, and showcases disruptive technologies.

Join us in advancing Ethiopia's journey towards a vibrant and sustainable digital future. STRIDE Ethiopia Expo 2024, May 18 - 26, 2024
